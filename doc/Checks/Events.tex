\subsection{Introduction}
\Glspl{Event} are triggers which the \gls{GM} may or may not care
about. And are usually used to effect \glsdisp{Character}{Character's}
\glspl{Trait}.

\subsection{Examples}

\sampleevent{Day}{Triggers when enough time has passed for the sun to rise}
\sampleevent{Night}{Triggers when enough time has passed for the sun to set}
